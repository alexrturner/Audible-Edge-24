Bio-long: <p>DJ Wayang (formerly Tran Scended), is an organiser, label head and DJ/radio host based in Boorloo/Perth. They are one half of the independent label and event series Pneumatic that has facilitated, alongside friends and visiting artists, a space to explore and experiment with sound in dance music. They also present regularly on RTRfms 'Rhythm Trippin' and continue to host club-focussed nights with the party series Mutual Affection.</p>
